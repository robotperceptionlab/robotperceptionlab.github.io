MITRO is the Mersey (formerly Maastricht) Intelligent Telepresence Robot, also known as Bob. The aim of the MITRO project is to develop a telepresence robot, allowing the user to be present in a remote location while maintaining mobility. We go beyond traditional telepresence robotics by bestowing the robot with intelligence, e.g. autonomous localisation and navigation, obstacle detection and avoidance, and people recognition and tracking.
