What's this all about?

RLP is a state-of-the-art robotics laboratory at the Department of Computer Science, University of Liverpool.  The lab is affiliated with the University’s Centre for Autonomous Systems Technology (CAST). The smARTLab team have been dedicated to the research in robot perception with vision and tactile sensing, and learning technologies (both single and multi-agent), with applications to robot grasping and manipulation.
